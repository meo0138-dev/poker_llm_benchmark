About the Benchmark

Methodology

Our benchmark evaluates LLMs on strategic poker reasoning through 20 carefully crafted scenarios. Each question tests different aspects of poker knowledge including hand evaluation, pot odds calculation, position play, and bluffing strategies. Models are evaluated on both accuracy and reasoning quality.

Evaluation Criteria

  • Correct action selection (fold/call/raise)
  • Accurate probability estimation
  • Sound strategic reasoning
  • Opponent modeling awareness
Note: The dataset is not publicly available to prevent contamination of future model training.
